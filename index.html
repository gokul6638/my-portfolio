My Projects

Vendor Selection & Fabric Sourcing Analysis

In this project, I evaluated multiple fabric suppliers for uniform manufacturing using a structured vendor selection approach. Suppliers were compared based on price, quality, MOQ, lead time, and reliability, supported by a weighted scoring model. I also analyzed the cost impact of each supplier choice. This project gave me hands-on exposure to procurement, sourcing decisions, and supply chain planning.

Logistics Cost and Transport Mode Analysis

This project involved comparing sea and air freight options for a garment export shipment. I calculated CBM, chargeable weight, total freight cost, and cost per unit to understand the impact of each transport mode. Based on cost efficiency and shipment nature, sea freight was selected as the most suitable option. The project helped me gain practical insight into freight calculation and logistics decision-making.

International Trade & Export Operations Simulation

In this project, I Prepared end-to-end export documents and logistics plan for a garment shipment from India to UAE, including costing, invoicing, freight calculation, and shipping decisions. Gained hands-on experience in international trade and export operations.
